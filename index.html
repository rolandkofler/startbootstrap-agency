Goals

of our Organization

Organize and steer the demand for blockchain consulting

The emerging field of blockchain technology has a skill squeeze problem. The technical know how is not readily available to serve the increasing client-base needs. The organisation will create a single point of contact to serve the client’s needs.

Support specializations in the field by joining forces

Independent consultants enjoy many liberties. They structure their work time and can often choose their clients and projects. Unfortunately they have limited resources and can not scale their workload. This is the tragedy of one-person-companies. Traditionally it is solved by creating business networks. The establishment of our organisation will be the consequence of translating this idea to the blockchain world.

Principles and Values

which guide the inception of our community

Distributed

Globally active and moving towards on-chain governance.

Autonomous

Independent from any person or group. Freedom of the individual and active participation.

Bottom up

From a sprout to a tree. The organisation will transform itself from a loose business network into a decentralized organisation.
